Foothold

Get your footing. Keep climbing.

Everything you need to use the Foothold logo on the course website (aishugithub.github.io/course_python), certificates, slides and social. Use the SVG files anywhere you can — they stay razor-sharp at every size. Use the PNG files for favicons, avatars and anything that can't take SVG.

Clear space & minimum size

  • Keep clear space around the mark equal to the height of one block (the small squares).
  • Don't show the icon smaller than 16px, or the full lockup smaller than 110px wide.

Do & don't

  • Do keep the amber block as the top step — it signals reaching the next foothold.
  • Do use the white version on navy or photos; the colour version on light/paper.
  • Don't recolour the mark outside the palette, stretch it, rotate it, or add shadows/gradients.
  • Don't retype "Foothold" in a different font — use the lockup files or Space Grotesk 600.

Questions or need another format (EPS/PDF for print, a social share banner, animated version)? Just ask.
